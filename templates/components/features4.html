How It Works

Loan Default Risk in Percentage

A precise calculation of the risk,  helping banks understand the likelihood of a customer defaulting on their loan effectively and facilitating better decision-making processes.
Feasible Loan Amount (in INR) Based on the customer's risk profile, we calculate the maximum loan amount that can be feasibly extended, ensuring that the bank’s lending decisions are based on reliable data.
Visual Analytics Dashboard We provide intuitive, interactive graphs and charts that summarize the loan’s risk, the feasibility of the amount, and other critical data points for a more visual and data-driven decision-making process.
